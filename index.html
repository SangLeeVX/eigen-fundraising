Market Timing Intelligence
💊
Zolbetuximab (Vyloy) FDA Approved
Oct 2024 — immediate market created
▶

Zolbetuximab became the first CLDN18.2-targeting therapy approved by FDA (Oct 2024) for gastric/GEJ adenocarcinoma. Every patient eligible for this drug requires companion diagnostic testing. That's an existing, growing clinical need — not a future one. AstraZeneca's MATTERHORN trial results in 2025 are expected to expand the eligible population further.

→ Lead with this in every investor conversation. It converts regulatory risk into market pull.

🔬
Liquid Biopsy CDx Market Timing
$4.5B+ by 2028, 23% CAGR
▶

Liquid biopsy companion diagnostics are the next frontier after tissue-based IHC. Foundation Medicine, Guardant, and Biodesix have proven the commercial model. The gap: none have a validated CLDN18.2 liquid biopsy CDx. The tissue-based IHC (standard of care) requires invasive biopsy, fails on heterogeneous tumors, and can't monitor treatment response. Liquid biopsy solves all three.

→ Eigen Bio is not entering a crowded market. It's entering a market that doesn't have a winner yet.

🌏
Korea / APAC — Asymmetric Advantage
Gastric cancer #1 in Korea; KSE network
▶

Korea has the highest gastric cancer incidence globally. MFDS regulatory pathway is faster than FDA for companion diagnostics with Korean-origin data. KSE network provides direct access to KAIST, Yonsei, SNU clinical collaborators. Samsung Medical, Asan Medical, Severance Hospital are all accessible validation sites.

→ Sang's Korean background is a structural advantage, not just a nice story. It's a faster path to clinical validation data.

The One-Line Pitch

Eigen Bio is building the first liquid biopsy companion diagnostic for CLDN18.2-positive gastric cancer — the exact biomarker that drives eligibility for Vyloy (zolbetuximab), FDA-approved October 2024 — with 100% concordance to tissue biopsy in peer-reviewed data.

Why Now

The FDA approval of Vyloy in October 2024 created a mandatory companion diagnostic market that doesn't exist yet. Every CLDN18.2+ gastric cancer patient eligible for Vyloy needs a diagnostic. The current standard — tissue IHC biopsy — is invasive, fails on tumor heterogeneity, and can't monitor treatment response. Liquid biopsy solves all three problems. No validated liquid biopsy CDx exists for CLDN18.2. Eigen Bio is building it.

The Data

  • 100% concordance with tissue biopsy IHC (n=10)
  • Spearman correlation r=0.76 — published in Analytical Chemistry, 2021
  • Peer-reviewed. Not internal data — an academic journal that peer-reviews methodology.
  • Pre-seed capital expands n to 50+ with MD Anderson as validation site

Market Size

  • ~1 million new gastric cancer diagnoses annually worldwide
  • ~25–30% are CLDN18.2-positive — the Vyloy-eligible population
  • Liquid biopsy CDx market: $4.5B+ by 2028, 23% CAGR
  • Korea alone: highest gastric cancer incidence globally — MFDS pathway faster than FDA

Competitive Moat

  • First-mover advantage: no validated liquid biopsy CDx for CLDN18.2 exists
  • Peer-reviewed concordance data is a 2-year head start vs. anyone starting now
  • KSE network provides access to Korean clinical sites (highest gastric cancer incidence)
  • MD Anderson collaboration (in negotiation) as US validation site
  • IP filing in progress — targeting the novel assay methodology

The Ask

Raising $50M Series A. Use of funds: expand clinical validation to n=50+ (MD Anderson), commission IP/FTO analysis, hire one clinical scientist, initiate MFDS pre-submission meeting in Korea. Milestone: validated assay data sufficient for pharma co-development conversation (AZ, Astellas) by Q4 2026.
