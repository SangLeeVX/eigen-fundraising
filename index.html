Market Timing Intelligence
🧬
Foundation Models Are Entering Biology
AlphaFold moment for RNA is now
▶

The field is converging on biology-native foundation models. EvolutionaryScale (ESM3), Arc Institute, Recursion, and Isomorphic Labs have all raised large rounds on this thesis. The gap: none are building causal supervision at scale. Prediction without interventional ground truth is brittle — this is the problem Eigen-1 is purpose-built to solve.

→ Frame Eigen Bio as the causal data layer that makes foundation models actually useful. The timing is exact.

📡
RNA as the Highest-Resolution Modality
Transcriptomics becoming the ground truth of drug development
▶

Single-cell and bulk RNA sequencing have become the de facto measurement standard in drug discovery. AstraZeneca, Novartis, and Genentech are all building RNA-based pharmacodynamic monitoring programs. Circulating RNA (cfRNA) is emerging as a real-time readout of drug mechanism. Eigen Bio's AZ BD conversation sits exactly at this inflection.

→ Eigen Bio is building the intelligence substrate that pharma will need to operate in an RNA-first world.

🌏
SF · Seoul · Singapore — Structural Advantage
Tri-city presence unlocks clinical data pipelines
▶

NUHS Singapore and MD Anderson are active clinical partners. Korea's scientific infrastructure (KAIST, POSTECH) and San Francisco's capital network create an asymmetric operating model most Western biotech AI companies can't replicate. The KSE network provides direct access to top Korean scientists — a talent pipeline that matters for wet lab scale-up.

→ Sang's multi-geography presence is a structural moat. It's faster, cheaper clinical data with credible institutional anchors.

The One-Line Pitch

Eigen Bio is a frontier wet AI lab building Eigen-1, a transcriptomic foundation model trained on RNA, biophysical data, and clinical data — with causal supervision generated from large-scale perturbation experiments across human organoids and cell lines.

The Core Bet

RNA is the highest-resolution measurement modality in biology. Whoever controls interventional ground truth at scale will define the next era of drug development. Most AI biology companies train on observational data. Observational models can predict — but prediction without causal grounding is brittle. Eigen Bio is building the experimental infrastructure to generate the kind of ground truth that makes a foundation model actually useful.

Why Now

  • Foundation models have proven themselves in protein structure (AlphaFold) and language — biology is next
  • Single-cell and spatial transcriptomics have made RNA the de facto measurement standard in drug discovery
  • Pharma is actively building RNA-based pharmacodynamic monitoring programs (AstraZeneca, Novartis, Genentech)
  • Causal data is the moat — no one at scale is combining wet lab perturbations with foundation model training

Traction

  • AstraZeneca BD conversation — pharmacodynamic monitoring via circulating RNA
  • NUHS Singapore — active clinical partnership
  • MD Anderson Cancer Center — active clinical partnership
  • Eigenfield: autonomous RNA regulatory knowledge graph, external-facing intelligence platform

The Vision

Eigen Bio becomes the intelligence substrate of medicine. Pharmaceutical companies and health systems operate as the execution layer on top of it. The model tells you what to make, who will respond, and why — at the RNA level, with causal evidence.

Competitive Moat

  • Causal supervision data from perturbation experiments — not available anywhere else at scale
  • Multi-modal training: RNA + biophysical data + clinical data in one model
  • Eigenfield knowledge graph as external platform and BD entry point
  • Tri-city presence (SF · Seoul · Singapore) — faster clinical data, asymmetric talent access
  • KSE network: direct access to KAIST, POSTECH, top Korean wet lab scientists

The Ask

Seed round targeting strategic family offices and institutional partners. Use of funds: scale perturbation experiments across organoid and cell line platforms, expand Eigen-1 training infrastructure, deepen AstraZeneca BD relationship, formalize NUHS and MD Anderson partnerships, grow SF and Seoul wet lab teams.
